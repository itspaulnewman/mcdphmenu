This meal combines two local favorites: McSpaghetti and Chicken McDo. The sweet-style spaghetti paired with crispy fried chicken is a comfort food that many Filipinos grew up with. It's a testament to McDonald's ability to adapt international dishes to local tastes

The iconic Big Mac remains a top choice among Filipinos. This two-patty burger featuring lettuce, cheese, pickles, onions, and special sauce is a global phenomenon that Filipinos love just as much as anyone else. Its enduring popularity is due to its satisfying portion and iconic status

4. Crispy Fish Fillet Sandwich

The Crispy Fish Fillet Sandwich has gained popularity as a seafood option at McDonald's Philippines. Its crispy fish patty, soft buns, and tartar sauce make it a hit among those seeking a seafood meal
